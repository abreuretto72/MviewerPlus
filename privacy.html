Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MviewerPlus ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We take your privacy seriously. MviewerPlus is a 100% free and open-source application that processes all files locally on your device and does not upload any of your data to external servers. We do not collect personal information, show no advertisements, and have no premium features or paywalls.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All files you open and view in MviewerPlus are processed entirely on your device. Your files, documents, and data:

  • Remain on your device at all times
  • Are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers
  • Are never transmitted over the internet
  • Are only accessible by you through the app

2. Permissions

Storage Access

Why we need this permission: MviewerPlus requires storage access to allow you to open, view, edit, and save files on your device.

The app uses the system file picker, which means:

  • You explicitly select which files the app can access
  • The app only reads files that you choose to open
  • No automatic scanning or indexing of your device storage
  • Files are processed locally on your device
  • Edited files are saved only when you explicitly choose to save them

Important: These permissions are used solely for local file operations. No data is transmitted outside your device unless you explicitly use the AI analysis feature (see below).

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All file processing occurs locally on your device
  • No cloud storage or remote servers are used for your files
  • The app does not require account creation or login
  • Your files remain under your complete control

5. Children's Privacy

MviewerPlus does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children specifically.
