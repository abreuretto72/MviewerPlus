Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how MviewerPlus ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We take your privacy seriously. MviewerPlus processes all files locally on your device and does not upload any of your data to external servers. We do not collect personal information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All files you open and view in MviewerPlus are processed entirely on your device. Your files, documents, and data:

  • Remain on your device at all times
  • Are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers
  • Are never transmitted over the internet
  • Are only accessible by you through the app

2. Permissions

Storage Access

The app requires storage permissions to:

  • Read files that you explicitly select to view
  • Save edited or exported files to your device
  • Access your device's file system for file browsing

Important: These permissions are used solely for local file operations. No data is transmitted outside your device.

AI Features (Optional)

If you choose to use the AI-powered file analysis feature:

  • File content may be sent to Google's Generative AI service for analysis
  • This is entirely optional and only occurs when you explicitly use the AI feature
  • You can use all other app features without ever using AI analysis
  • AI analysis is subject to Google's privacy policy

3. Third-Party Services

Free Version

If you use the free version of MviewerPlus, we may display advertisements through third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob). These services may:

  • Collect device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • Use cookies and similar technologies
  • Show personalized advertisements based on your interests

For more information about how these services handle your data, please refer to:

  • Google Privacy Policy

Premium Version

The Premium version of MviewerPlus:

  • Contains no advertisements
  • Does not use any advertising services
  • Provides an ad-free experience

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All file processing occurs locally on your device
  • No cloud storage or remote servers are used for your files
  • The app does not require account creation or login
  • Your files remain under your complete control

5. Children's Privacy

MviewerPlus does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children specifically.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no data for us to delete, modify, or provide. You have complete control over your files on your device.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub: Open an Issue
  • Repository: MviewerPlus on GitHub
